About ThinkBin

ThinkBin helps small and medium businesses map, optimize, and document processes in plain language. SimulMap builds a map while teaching best practices, then generates SOPs, KPI trackers, and knowledge pages.

  • Core — Process mapping + optimization + auto-docs.
  • Learn — LMS from your processes: courses, quizzes, enrollments.
  • Start — Freemium onramp for new founders to turn ideas into an operating map.
